                    Hello, my name is Minyahel Fekadu. I'm a software engineering student at Addis 
Ababa University. Besides that, i'm 
                    an avid sports lover and a casual gamer.
                     
                    
Software Engineer
 I have always been interested in computers and anything related to them since I was 
                    a little kid. But, apart from just fiddling and playing with them I didn't really have any 
                    coding experience until I entered the 9th grade. But from then on, my knowledge of programming has grown
                    steadly. The first programming language I learned was C++ in highschool. Altough I don't remember much of it now,
                    I can credit nearly all of my software engineering interest to it since it showed me how fun and interesting
                    programming can be. In my first year as a software engineering student, I learned python which I beleive is 
                    a great programming language for begginers. Following that, in my second year, I learned Java. Apart from being 
                    one of the most popular programming language in the world, I found Java and still find Java to be a lot of fun.

                    
My Blood is Red
 
                    


                    
Since I started watching sports from a
                    young age, I have been interested in one and only one team, The Reds, Manchester United. Over the years watching 
                    United play became one of my favorite passtimes and eventually evolved from being just a hobby to a culture.I cherish the 90 minutes 
                    of fun, sad and sometimes outright boring games and they have given me a greater connection with my father and brother who are also

Gamers Unite
 
                    
Playing games is one of my favorite recreational activities. From an early age, I liked gaming like any child would
                    but as time went on I found games as a way to let my creativity loose. Instead of the old and boring first person shooter games, 
                    I am much more into sandbox that allow the player to explore and create anything of their liking. These kind of games test a 
                    persons creativity and help exercise the mind. There are many games in this genre and I've tried out many of them but below are some
                    of the games that I really enjoyed

                    

                        
Minecraft

                        

                        
Arguably one of the most addictive games ever created, it's pixelated and endless procedurally-generated world will have you up late at night 
                        trying to recreate the pyramid of giza


                        
BeamNG.drive

                        
An open world driving simulator with a soft-body physics engine unlike any other. This game has realistic damage to vehicles and will surely
                        be a favorite for those back-yard scientists


                        
No Man's Sky

                        

                        
Yet another procedurally generated open world game only this time, the world is the universe consisting of millions of stars and their planets. Definitely
                        recommended for those who just can't help but look up to the sky at night

                    

                    
The games above are what I would consider the best games ever and I would recommend anyone looking for a challenge to try them out.
