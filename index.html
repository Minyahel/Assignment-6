Apart from being one of the most popular programming language in the world, I found Java and still find Java to be a lot of fun.

Gamers Unite

Playing games is one of my favorite recreational activities. From an early age, I liked gaming like any child would but as time went on I found games as a way to let my creativity loose. Instead of the old and boring first person shooter games, I am much more into sandbox that allow the player to explore and create anything of their liking. These kind of games test a persons creativity and help exercise the mind. There are many games in this genre and I've tried out many of them but below are some of the games that I really enjoyed

Minecraft

Arguably one of the most addictive games ever created, it's pixelated and endless procedurally-generated world will have you up late at night trying to recreate the pyramid of giza


BeamNG.drive

An open world driving simulator with a soft-body physics engine unlike any other. This game has realistic damage to vehicles and will surely be a favorite for those back-yard scientists


No Man's Sky

Yet another procedurally generated open world game only this time, the world is the universe consisting of millions of stars and their planets. Definitely recommended for those who just can't help but look up to the sky at night

The games above are what I would consider the best games ever and I would recommend anyone looking for a challenge to try them out.
